I’m a data scientist with a background in technology and management consulting. I have experience leading high visibility technical projects from conception to completion; with hundreds of daily users accessing data products that were built by my team and I. I’ve worked in a variety of industries ranging from consulting to healthcare to e-commerce and am quick to learn how a business operates. I look forward to continue working on interesting projects and welcome opportunities for collaboration within the data science space.
