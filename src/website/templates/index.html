Artist Recommender

January 2nd, 2017

I rely on streaming services such as Soundcloud and Spotify to get new recommendations on House music to listen to. One shortcoming of these recommender system is that they only recommend individual songs at a time. I realized I would find it valuable to explore entire artist recommendations so as to explore music that I like in larger quantities for a more immersive experience than one-off songs from a variety of artists that I may or may not like.

Visit salstuff.com/artistrecommender to navigate to the artist recommender.

For a breakdown of how the recommender was built, visit github.com/salmank09/artistrecommender.
